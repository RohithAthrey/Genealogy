Clan Origin: It is assumed that when Kabaaka Kintu (the founding King of Buganda) came to Buganda, he came along with Ssessanga as one of his followers, who was carrying Kintu's spears and shields. When Kintu settled at Nnono, he awarded Ssessanga a place at a village called Ntonyeze in Busujju. While at Ntonyeze Ssessanga had a son, called Mukalo. When Ssessanga died, Mukalo became his heir. King Ccwal transferred Mukalo from Ntonyeze and gave him a place at Kambugu, in Busiro. Since that time Kambugu is the seat (Butaka) of the Njovu clan. Mukalo is the founding father of the clan.
